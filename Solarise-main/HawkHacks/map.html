Total Output:

  • Max Module: This represents the highest power output a solar panel can generate under ideal conditions, helping buyers understand the panel's peak performance potential.

  • Annual Sunshine: It signifies the average amount of sunlight received per day in a specific location, aiding in estimating the solar energy generation potential throughout the year for accurate system sizing.

  • Roof Area: Refers to the available space on a roof where solar panels can be installed, crucial for determining the number of panels that can be accommodated to maximize energy production.

  • Module Output: It reflects the actual power output of solar panels under real-world conditions, accounting for factors like temperature and shading to provide a more accurate estimation of energy production.

  • Total Output: Represents the cumulative energy production of the solar panel system over a specific period, essential for evaluating the system's performance, return on investment, and contribution to energy savings and sustainability goals.
